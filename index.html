Hi there!


My name is Dominique and in my arsenal are my many years of customer service and administrative experience that I believe translate very well into programming.


In February 2021, I decided to leave my full-time job as Project Manager and Customer Service Manager (yes, I held two roles!) for an e-commerce company to fully dive into the world of coding. I enrolled in Coding Dojo's Fullstack Web Development Certificate program and completely immersed myself into learning different languages for three months. I was pretty much living and breathing code, and it was at this time that I fell in love with coding. There is so much to learn- it's like a gift that keeps on giving!


You might ask how I can use my customer service and administrative skills in this industry, and I am more than glad to share. If you would like to hear more about my journey, please do not hesitate to reach out to me via email.
